About US

Kohli Company founded in 2006 is a group of businesses, that develop innovative Web-Products serving people worldwide, is now Kohli Media LLP - an Indian Private Limited Company.

Kohli Businesses are the fastest growing businesses worldwide in their corresponding industry segments. The combined Product Portfolio of various Kohli' businesses includes - Web & Desktop Applications, Call Center Services, Web Services: Domain Regsitration and Web Hosting, SMS Gateway, Hosted IVR Solutions, Social Networking Software, Internet & Outdoor Advertising, Online Advertising Solutions, Brand Marketing, Event Management and much more.

All our businesses have been profitable from inception. All assets of the company are organically funded without any external debt or borrowing.
